Detailed Description

A mutable string, that provides public access to its internal buffer and fields and does not implement thread synchronization. Unfortunately, the given C#/JAVA StringBuilder/Buffer classes are "sealed" and do not provide direct access to the internal buffer. Hence, to avoid conversion to new immutable strings in certain situations, we rebuild a portion of the StringBuilder functionality here. More complex functions, like C# Format() operations are not supported and remain the domain of String and StringBuffer classes.
